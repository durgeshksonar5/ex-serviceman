Advanced Fire Safety Training & Equipment Audits

Fire safety is not just an option; it's a critical legal and moral obligation for any organization. At Ex-Serviceman Security & Manpower Agency, we provide comprehensive fire safety solutions that empower your workforce to handle emergencies with calm and precision.

Our program is led by ex-defense personnel and certified safety experts who bring real-world experience to the training room. From basic awareness to complex evacuation drills, we ensure your premises are not just compliant, but truly protected.

Why Choose Our Fire Safety Program?

  • Certified Instructors: Training led by ex-military and fire safety specialists.
  • Practical Hands-on Drills: Exercises involving the use of multiple types of extinguishers and hydrants.
  • Compliance & Auditing: Regular equipment checks to ensure your facility meets regional fire norms.
  • Customized SOPs: Standard Operating Procedures tailored to your specific building layout.
  • Emergency Signage: Assistance in planning and installing critical safety and exit signage.
